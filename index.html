Questions

Frequently asked

Do I need prior coding experience? +
No. Our programs start from the basics. You need curiosity and consistency — we provide the structure and mentorship.
Is this a live class or self-paced? +
It's cohort-based with weekly project milestones. You work mostly async but have live check-ins with mentors every week. Best of both worlds.
What is the internship certificate? +
On completing your program, you receive a digitally signed iKalvi internship certificate. It's verifiable online and formatted to be added directly to your LinkedIn and resume.
Is there a refund policy? +
We offer a 7-day no-questions-asked refund if you feel the program isn't right for you. After week 1, refunds are not available.
Who are the mentors? +
Mentors are working software engineers and designers with 3–10 years of experience at Indian startups and product companies. They volunteer their time because they believe in the mission.
